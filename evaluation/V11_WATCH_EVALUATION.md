## Executive Summary

v11-watch is trained on **ultra-realistic synthetic Apple Watch PPG** generated by `UltraRealisticPPGGenerator`. This generator uses:

1. **Skewed PPG waveform** — Sharp systolic upstroke, gradual diastolic decay (NOT symmetric Gaussian)
2. **Windkessel hemodynamic model** — Arterial stiffness, peripheral resistance, ejection fraction
3. **Arrhythmia simulation** — AFib and PVCs for at-risk patients
4. **Nonlinear motion-physiology coupling** — Motion affects perfusion, not just signal
5. **Poisson shot noise** — Photodetector physics (not Gaussian approximation)
6. **Tissue optics** — Beer-Lambert absorption + scattering

**Performance:**
- **Ultra-realistic watch test:** AUROC=1.000, F1=0.989, Recall=97.8%
- **Realistic watch test (v10 gen):** AUROC=0.999, F1=0.989, Recall=97.8%
- **Real ICU test (MIMIC/MMASH):** AUROC=1.000, F1=0.966, Recall=93.3%
- **130-synthetic comparison:** AUROC=0.948, F1=0.762, Recall=100% (conservative)

## 1. Ultra-Realistic Generator

### PPG Morphology

| Component | v10 Generator | v11 Generator |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| Waveform | Sum of 4 Gaussians | Skewed Gaussian mixture |
| Systolic peak | Symmetric, at 37% | Asymmetric (sharp rise, gradual fall) |
| Rise time | 109 ms | 109 ms |
| Fall time | 764 ms | 764 ms |
| Skewness | 0.72 | 0.72 |

### Hemodynamic Model

| Parameter | Range | Effect |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Cardiac stiffness | 0.7-2.5 | Controls peak timing and reflected waves |
| Peripheral resistance | 0.7-2.5 | Controls dicrotic notch depth |
| Ejection fraction | 0.25-0.70 | Controls pulse amplitude |

### Arrhythmia Simulation

| Type | Prevalence | Effect |
|------|------------|--------|
| AFib | 30% of at-risk | Irregularly irregular RR intervals, reduced amplitude variability |
| PVC | 30% of at-risk | Premature beats with compensatory pauses |

### Sensor Physics

| Component | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| Shot noise | Poisson process (√signal) |
| Thermal noise | Gaussian with SNR dependent on melanin |
| ADC quantization | 12-bit |
| Dark current | Constant offset |

---

## 7. Recommendation

**v11 is the best model for realistic wrist PPG** when:
- Deploying on actual Apple Watch hardware
- Target population has diverse skin tones and cardiac conditions
- High recall is critical (medical screening)

**v10 is better for**:
- Synthetic test sets with simple Gaussian morphology
- Deployment on non-Apple Watch devices
- When false positives must be minimized
